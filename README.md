 Berkeley Schedule Planner

 Berkeley Bear Schedule Planner is a lightweight front-end prototype for planning across a wider range of UC Berkeley majors. It turns a student's major, starting point, domain emphasis when relevant, hobbies, AP credit, summer preference, optional research or co-op term, and secondary program goals into a semester-by-semester plan.

## What it does

- Builds an 8-semester roadmap automatically.
- Supports a wider Berkeley major set including Data Science, Computer Science, Statistics, Cognitive Science, Economics, Political Science, English, Molecular and Cell Biology, and Mechanical Engineering.
- Includes a Data Science domain-emphasis selector.
- Lets students mark AP or transfer credit as already fulfilled.
- Adds optional summer terms plus optional gap, research, or co-op terms.
- Adds Berkeley-focused secondary program planning for minors and double-major tracks.
- Limits technical course stacking based on student preference.
- Surfaces registration insights, requirement progress, and support recommendations.
